Professional Profile

Bridging rigorous construction practices with long-term public utility management.

With over 15 years of combined experience in civil engineering and public water infrastructure, Md.   • Unwavering focus on structural reliability and public safety compliance.
  • Expert coordination with government agencies, vendors, and private contractors.
  • Commitment to sustainable urban water supply development.
  • Proven track record of zero-downtime maintenance on critical water assets.
